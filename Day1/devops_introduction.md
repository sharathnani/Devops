# Devops

Devops is a process of improving the application delivery by ensuring the proper automation, Quality, Continuous monitoring and Continuous testing in place.
Devops is a culture that improve the delivery of application.

# How to introduce youself:
* Hi, my name is Sharath. I’m a DevOps Engineer with hands-on experience in automating, deploying, and maintaining scalable infrastructure and CI/CD pipelines. My day-to-day responsibilities revolve around ensuring that development and operations teams collaborate efficiently, leading to faster and more reliable software delivery

# day to day activities:
* Verify the Jira Dashboard and Check the tickets and check the priority
* Verify the alerts and monitor the application dash boards
* Stand-ups & Meetings: Participate in daily stand-ups with dev/ops teams to align priorities and address bottlenecks.
* Pipeline Management: Design, maintain, and optimize CI/CD pipelines (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI) for faster, reliable deployments.
* System Health Checks: Monitor dashboards (e.g., Grafana, Prometheus) and logs (ELK Stack) to preempt issues.
* On-Call & Troubleshooting: Respond to alerts, resolve outages, and conduct post-mortems to document root causes and fixes.
* Security & Compliance: Implement security practices (e.g., secret management with Vault, vulnerability scanning in pipelines).
* Process Optimization: Refine deployment strategies, reduce downtime, and enhance system reliability.
* Knowledge Sharing: Maintain runbooks, update deployment procedures, and ensure clarity for team processes.
* Performing log analysis and debugging issues in dev/staging/production environments
